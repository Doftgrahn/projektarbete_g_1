Mapei Jacqueline Mapei Cummings (born December 20, 1983), known by her stage name Mapei, is a Swedish-American recording artist best known for her single "Don't Wait", which was released via Downtown Records in 2013. Influenced by her British father's musical heritage and her Finnish mother's melancholy, Miriam Bryant began to write songs in November 2011 together with her childhood friend, Victor Rådström who is a producer and songwriter. Four months later, she produced three songs released in her single debut "Finders, Keepers" in March 2012 through the record label 100 songs[1]. The song received worldwide attention among bloggers and music sites.[citation needed] She's signed to Warner Music in Sweden and to Interscope Records in the United States.
